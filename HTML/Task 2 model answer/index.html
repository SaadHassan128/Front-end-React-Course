About My Teaching

As a Full Stack Web Development Instructor and Head of Software Department at Academy of Digital Arts Egypt, I specialize in teaching modern web technologies to the next generation of developers. My courses focus on practical, hands-on learning with real-world projects.

Key Teaching Areas:

  • HTML5 & CSS3 with responsive design
  • JavaScript (ES6+) and modern frameworks
  • Backend development with Node.js and Express
  • Database design and management
  • Full stack project development
  • Software architecture and best practices
