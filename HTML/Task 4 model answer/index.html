Breaking News

Academy of Digital Arts Egypt Launches Advanced Full Stack Program

December 17, 2024

Academy of Digital Arts Egypt has announced the launch of its most comprehensive full stack web development program to date. Led by Head of Software Department Saad Hassan Saad, the program covers modern technologies including React, Node.js, and advanced database design. The 6-month intensive course aims to prepare students for high-demand developer positions in Egypt's growing tech sector.

Latest Technology News

Egypt's Tech Sector Shows 40% Growth in Web Development Jobs

December 16, 2024

A new report from Egypt's Ministry of Communications and Information Technology reveals that web development positions have increased by 40% in the past year. This growth is attributed to the digital transformation initiatives across various industries and the increasing demand for online services. Local educational institutions like ADA Egypt are responding by expanding their programming curricula to meet market demands.

JavaScript Remains Top Programming Language in Egyptian Job Market

December 15, 2024

According to the latest developer survey conducted across major Egyptian cities, JavaScript continues to dominate as the most in-demand programming language. Full stack developers with expertise in React and Node.js command the highest salaries, with average compensation reaching 25,000 EGP monthly for experienced professionals. This trend aligns with global market demands and positions Egyptian developers competitively in the international market.

New Web Development Bootcamp Standards Introduced in Egyptian Universities

December 14, 2024

The Egyptian Ministry of Higher Education has approved new standards for web development education programs across universities and technical institutes. The standards emphasize practical, project-based learning and require students to complete at least three full stack applications before graduation. Industry experts, including instructors from ADA Egypt, contributed to developing these comprehensive guidelines.

Remote Work Opportunities Expand for Egyptian Developers

December 13, 2024

Egyptian web developers are increasingly securing remote positions with international companies, with a 60% increase in remote job placements this year. This trend is facilitated by improved internet infrastructure and the high quality of technical education provided by institutions like Academy of Digital Arts Egypt. Many graduates are now working for European and American tech companies while remaining based in Egypt.
